1) Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Numerix Simple (the "App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you install, access, or use the App and related services. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

This document is written for a U.S. audience in plain, American English.

2) Information We Collect

We follow a data minimization approach and collect only what is necessary to operate and improve the App.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account details (e.g., display name, email address) if you create an account or contact us.
  • Support messages, feedback, and other content you submit.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device data: device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, and general system settings.
  • Logs and diagnostics: crash reports, performance metrics, and usage statistics (e.g., feature taps, session length) to improve stability and quality.
  • Network information: connection type and status (not the content of your communications).

We do not access sensitive device data (e.g., precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or payment card numbers) unless a feature requires it and you grant permission.

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App’s core features.
  • Fix bugs, analyze performance, and enhance security and reliability.
  • Research and develop new features using aggregated and de-identified information.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

4) Device Permissions

Some features may request device permissions. We will ask before accessing them, and you can change your choice at any time in your device settings. The current version does not require by default the following sensitive permissions:

  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Camera, Photos/Media, Microphone
  • Push Notifications

If future features need these permissions, we will request your consent again and update this Policy.

5) Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information in these limited cases:

  • Service providers. Vendors that help us with hosting, analytics, diagnostics, or customer support. They may process data only on our instructions and must protect it.
  • Legal and safety. To comply with laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable government requests; to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale. We will notify you, where feasible, and ensure the recipient upholds comparable protections.

6) Ads, Analytics & Tracking

At this time, the App does not use targeted advertising SDKs and does not track users across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.

We may use basic analytics (e.g., crash and performance diagnostics) to improve functionality. Where required by law, we will obtain consent before using optional analytics or introduce an in‑App choice.

7) Data Security & Retention

  • Security. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (e.g., access controls, encryption in transit, least-privilege access) to protect information.
  • Retention. We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law, then delete or de‑identify it.
  • Incident response. If a security incident occurs, we will take appropriate steps and notify you and/or regulators when legally required.

8) Your Privacy Choices & Rights

8.1 Choices

  • Account & communications. You can update or delete account details (where available) and adjust communication preferences.
  • Permissions. You can revoke device permissions in system settings; some features may stop working without them.

8.2 U.S. State Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, in California, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Virginia, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws), you may have the right to:

  • Request to know/access and obtain a copy of personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Request information about our data practices (categories of data collected, sources, purposes, and disclosures).
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information and certain targeted advertising or profiling, where applicable.
  • Appeal a denial of your request (for states that provide an appeal right).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will verify your request and respond within the time required by law. Authorized agents may submit requests when supported by proof of authorization and identity verification.

8.3 California Notices (CCPA/CPRA)

  • No sale/share. We do not sell or share personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising. We do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of consumers under 16 years of age.
  • Sensitive personal information. We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes requiring a right to limit under California law.
  • Non‑discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

9) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or a higher age as required by your state). If we learn that such information was collected, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe a child provided information may contact us.

10) International Transfers

Your information may be processed outside your state or country. When applicable, we will implement appropriate safeguards required by law.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will provide notice in the App or by other reasonable means. Your continued use of the App after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
