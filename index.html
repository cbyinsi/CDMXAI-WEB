Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Numerix Simple (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1) Information We Collect

We aim to collect only what is necessary to operate and improve the App.

  • Information you provide: numeric inputs, saved items (e.g., favorites, history), feedback you send us via email.
  • Device & usage data (non-identifying): device model, operating system, app version, basic event logs, and diagnostics (e.g., crashes, performance metrics).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (e.g., government IDs, health, biometric, or precise geolocation) and do not create user profiles for advertising.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide core features (number conversion, number facts, math properties).
  • To improve stability, performance, and user experience.
  • To fix bugs, analyze crashes, and prevent abuse.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.

3) Processing on Your Device

Many calculations and transformations occur locally on your device. Where cloud services are used (if any), we limit transmission to what is required for the feature to work.

5) Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law. You may request deletion as described in Your Rights.

6) Children’s Privacy
